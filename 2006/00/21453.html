II.2) QUANTITY OR SCOPE OF THE CONTRACT

II.2.1) Total quantity or scope (including all lots and options, if applicable)
LOT 1:
– Assessing the expert activities of the document laboratory and presenting a survey on the results of the assessment.
– Developing the methods and means of the document examining activity in order to establish the possibility of carrying out this activity under quality assured conditions.
– Organizing a visit to a document examining laboratory of an ENFSI member institute that applies quality assurance system.
– Elaborating a work plan to reorganise the expert examination system, according to the MSZ EN ISO 17025:2001 standard in order to enable it to obtain the national accredited status.
LOT 2:
– Providing information on the examination methods applied by explosives laboratories of ENFSI member institutions, as well as on concepts related to the elaboration of quality assurance systems.
– Introducing the principles that intend to uniform the analytical methods and quality assurance systems at international level.
– Visiting of an explosives laboratory of an ENFSI member institute.
– Assessing the requirements of being a member of the unified European explosive analytical system, which is under foundation.
